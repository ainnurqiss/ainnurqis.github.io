About Me

Beyond academics, I love coding personal projects, solving coding challenges, and designing UI prototypes. I find UI/UX design fascinating, as it allows me to create intuitive and visually appealing interfaces. I also enjoy participating in hackathons, exploring new technologies, and improving my problem-solving skills. In my free time, I enjoy reading novels and manhwas as it allows me to explore different worlds, ideas, and perspectives. Whether it's fiction, mystery, or sci-fi, reading helps me relax and enhances my creativity. Outside of tech and reading, I have a passion for gaming, photography, and music. Gaming helps me unwind and think strategically, while photography allows me to capture creativity in a different form. Music is another big part of my life, providing both relaxation and inspiration.
